Big-O Notation:
Time is an important aspect in computer science. How fast a program can run can be a decisive factor in whether or not a client will purchase your product or users will love it. It is very important to make your program as efficient as possible, which is where the Big-O notation comes in. Big-O notation plays a big part in computer science because it describes how fast a certain program or operation will run. The link above will show all of the Big-O notations for many of the common complexities and algorithms that can be found in computer science. Personally, I strive to keep up to date with this knowledge and to memorize them in order to efficiently write programs that run as fast as possible.
