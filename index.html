Designed and created an Android App with a team of 3 others during UC San Diego's first hackathon: SDHacks. The purpose of the app is to dissuade students from using their phone at all while studying. We made use of the Android's built in accelorometer so that when the phone is picked up, it will simultaneously vibrate, blink its flashlight and play a siren noise until it is placed back down on the table. I was in charge of the back end portion of this app designing the algorithm to allow the phone to sense when it is picked up as well as make it function as specified when picked up.
